Sushi Rice

Description

This delicious Japanese rice is perfect for all styles of sushi and maki roll, or even on it's own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked short grain rice(sushi rice)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. rinse the rice in a strainer under cold water until water runs clear.
  3. combine rice and water into saucepan over med-high heat and boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook until rice is tender and all water is aborbed, 20 minutes. Remove from stove and let cool.
  4. Meanwhile, combine vinegar, oil, sugar, salt over med heat. Cook until sugar dissolves, then cool. Stir into cooked rice until dry and cool to handle.
  5. Enjoy!
